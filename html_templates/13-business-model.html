Dual market revenue streams optimized for market fit

DEPLOYMENT LICENSE_

IMPLEMENTATION SERVICES_

$50,000 - $150,000

ENTERPRISE INTEGRATIONS_

$75,000 - $200,000

COMMERCIAL MARKET_

1

SaaS SUBSCRIPTION_

Tiered SaaS pricing model based on user count, transaction volume, and features. Billed annually with enterprise discounts.

$30,000 - $250,000 / year
2

TRANSACTION FEES_

Shell Token supply chain financing with 0.5% transaction fee for rapid supplier payments. Based on transaction volume.

0.5% of payment volume
3

PARTNER INTEGRATIONS_

Pre-built integrations with major ERP systems and supply chain platforms. Licensed as add-ons to base subscription.

$10,000 - $50,000 / year
