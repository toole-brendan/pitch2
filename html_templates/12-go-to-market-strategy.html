Go-To-Market Strategy

DEFENSE_STRATEGY

1

DIRECT SALES_

Focus on direct engagement with battalion and brigade-level leadership to secure pilot programs. Leverage past performance metrics from existing deployments as proof points.

2

CHANNEL PARTNERS_

Partner with established defense contractors to include HandReceipt as part of larger digital transformation initiatives. Target systems integrators with existing DoD relationships.

3

CONTRACT VEHICLES_

Secure positions on DoD and GSA contract vehicles to streamline procurement process. Target SBIR Phase III transitions for direct-to-production contracts.

4

EXPANSION STRATEGY_

Establish success within Army units, then expand to other service branches. Use successful implementations to target enterprise-level contracts with Defense Logistics Agency.

COMMERCIAL_STRATEGY

1

VERTICAL FOCUS_

Initial targeting of high-value inventory sectors: electronics manufacturing, luxury goods, and industrial equipment. Develop industry-specific marketing materials and case studies.

2

SALES APPROACH_

Combination of inside sales team for SMB market and field sales for enterprise clients. Implement ROI calculator to demonstrate clear value proposition during sales process.

3

PARTNERSHIPS_

Form strategic alliances with ERP software providers, industry associations, and blockchain infrastructure companies to expand market reach and technical capabilities.

4

MARKETING CHANNELS_

Focus on industry-specific conferences, targeted digital marketing campaigns, and thought leadership content. Develop demo program showing tangible benefits in client environments.
